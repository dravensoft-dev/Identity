01 — The symbol

The Rotor

Three identical blades spinning around a shared axis. An original abstraction of the spinning-axe gesture: speed, precision and control. No literal weapon — pure geometry in perpetual motion.

  • 01120° rotationThe three blades repeat in perfect radial symmetry, creating balance and a sense of continuous spin.
  • 02Asymmetric edgeEach blade combines a straight edge and a beveled one — evoking the cut without depicting a real axe.
  • 03Empty central axisThe meeting point stays open: the focus is the motion, not the center.
  • 04A single weightThe symbol works in positive, negative and monochrome without losing identity.
